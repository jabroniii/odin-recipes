Boiled Goose

How to make it

Boiled goose is an evergreen dish in any household

Call the kids, because this will be a treat

For this recipe you will need

  • A large caserole
  • Water
  • A goose

Follow these steps in order

  1. Pour water in caserole, put it on a hot stove and wait for it to boil
  2. Put 2 kg of salt in the water
  3. Boil the goose for 39 hours or until you feel like it is ready

Enjoy!
