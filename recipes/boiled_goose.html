Lasagna

How to make it

Lasagna is easy to love but difficult to make.

Do not underestimate the complexity of simplicity.

For this recipe you will need

  • A microoven or oven
  • Electricty
  • 8 USD or a something to trade with
  • Lasagna
  • A pair of eyes not a must

Follow these steps in order

  1. Exchange currency for lasagna
  2. Check oven or microoven and thus electricity
  3. Cook lasagna per instructions on the box

Enjoy!
